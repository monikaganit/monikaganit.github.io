About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Hampton University. My research interests lie in Functional Analysis and Operator Theory, with particular emphasis on Banach space geometry, numerical index theory, Bishop–Phelps–Bollobás type properties, tensor product spaces, and operator theory.

Research Interests

  • Functional Analysis
  • Operator Theory
  • Banach Space Geometry
  • Numerical Index Theory
  • Bishop–Phelps–Bollobás Properties
  • Tensor Product Spaces
