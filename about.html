About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Hampton University. My research interests lie in Functional Analysis and Operator Theory, with particular emphasis on Banach space geometry, numerical index theory, Bishop–Phelps–Bollobás type properties, tensor product spaces, and related questions in operator theory.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Mathematics, University of Memphis
  • M.Sc. in Mathematics, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay
  • B.Sc. in Mathematics, University of Delhi
