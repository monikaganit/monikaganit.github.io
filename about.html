About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Mathematics at Hampton University. Before joining Hampton University, I was a Lecturer in Mathematics at Stevens Institute of Technology.

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Memphis, where my advisors were Fernanda Botelho and Bentuo Zheng. I completed my M.Sc. in Mathematics at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay and my undergraduate degree in Mathematics at the University of Delhi.

My research interests are in Functional Analysis and Operator Theory, especially Banach space geometry, numerical index theory, Bishop–Phelps–Bollobás type properties, tensor product spaces, quotient lifting properties, and algebraic and spectral properties of operators.
